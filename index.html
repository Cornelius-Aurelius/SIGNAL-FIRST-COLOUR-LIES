How to play

Find Proof, Not Colour

The screen tries to trick you. Your job is to find the true signal before you tap.

1. Read the target signal
Example: 3 dots + round border. That is the truth you are hunting.
2. Ignore colour first
Colour may look right but still be a trap. Do not trust it alone.
3. Ignore lying words
A card might say RED, GREEN or BLUE. The word can be bait.
4. Check the proof
Look for dots, pattern, border, number, shape and badge position.
5. Tap only true matches
Correct signal = Signal Hit. Correct signal through bait = Trap Beaten.
